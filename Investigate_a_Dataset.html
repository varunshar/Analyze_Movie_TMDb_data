import pprint
#defining the function
def calculate(column):
    #for highest earned profit
    high= TMDb_data[column].idxmax()
    high_details=pd.DataFrame(TMDb_data.loc[high])
    
    #for lowest earned profit
    low= TMDb_data[column].idxmin()
    low_details=pd.DataFrame(TMDb_data.loc[low])
    
    #To concatenate data in one place
    info=pd.concat([high_details, low_details], axis=1)
    
    return info

Conclusions¶

From all the results found above conclusion for profitable movies can be made that :

  • Genre of movie should be Comedy (1st), Drama (2nd) or Action (3rd)
  • Cast can be chosen from Tom Cruise, Brad Pitt, Tom Hanks, Sylvester Stallone, Cameron Diaz
  • Preferable Month for Movie release date are June, December, May, November, July
  • The average budget for profitable movie around 60 million dollar.
  • The average runtime for profitable movie around 113 mins.
  • Directors can be chosen from Steven Spielberg, Robert Zemeckis, Clint Eastwood as most experienced ones.

Limitations¶

We should keep in mind that above analysis is only pointing towards the characteristics for higher chances of profitabilty. The dataset provided to analyze is time-bound and trendz do change over period of time. Also, in dataset currency is not defined so we are assuming the profits are in USD but may be it also includes profits from other countries and currencies as well. Therefore, above characteristics are helpful to generate people's attraction to movie and therefore results in high profitabilty. Other than this, there are also other important influential factors like story,movie marketing etc on which profitability depends.
